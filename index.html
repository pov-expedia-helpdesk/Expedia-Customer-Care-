09 Ways to Call Expedia Customer Service – Phone, Email or Chat

Expedia’s main customer service number is +1-888 (829) 1117 [US-Expedia OTA Live Person], available 24/7.

Whether you're changing bookings, canceling flights, or requesting a refund, contacting a live person ensures a quick resolution. You can also use Expedia's live chat or email options for assistance.

Why Contact a Live Person at Expedia?

  • Flight changes or cancellations: Update or cancel your trip efficiently.
  • Booking clarification: Understand details of your reservation.
  • Refunds and compensation: Handle complex refund issues with a real person.
  • Technical glitches: Resolve payment or system errors directly.

Ways to Reach Expedia Customer Support

1. Phone Support

Call +1-888 (829) 1117 anytime. Say “agent” during the prompts for faster access.

2. Live Chat

Go to the Expedia Help Center and click on “Live Chat”.

3. Email Support

Use email for non-urgent matters and to maintain a written record of your issue.

4. Expedia App

Chat or call customer service directly through the app.

5. Help Center

Search FAQs and get instant help at Expedia Help Center.

6. Social Media

Message Expedia via Facebook or Twitter for general inquiries. Don’t post personal information.

7. Contact Form

Fill out the “Contact Us” form for a response within 24–48 hours.

8. International Support

Call +1-888 (829) 1117 from anywhere for international inquiries.

9. Use Your Travel Agent

If you booked through a travel agent, they can help you contact Expedia support.

Tips for Faster Service
